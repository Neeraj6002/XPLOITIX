ABOUT THE EVENT

XPLOITIX is a flagship cybersecurity hands-on workshop organized by IEEE SB MCET × Offenso, aimed at empowering students with practical, real-world security skills.

This event is designed to take participants beyond classroom theory and into the actual working environment of cybersecurity, where learning goes far beyond observing tools or memorizing concepts. At XPLOITIX, participants explore how cybersecurity solutions are designed, developed, and implemented from the ground up.
